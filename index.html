      How to Play the Game

      • Our main character is a ninja in search for gold. He can find gold in 4 different places: the cave, the house, the gold mine and at the casino.
      • The player will choose between the 4 places.
      • By clicking the button above the place, A pop-up will appear and display what happened.
      • The player has 20 moves to gain gold.
      • To win the game, the player must get a total of 250 gold.

      Probability and Breakdown

      • cave 100% + 5 gold
      • house 80% + 10 gold
      • gold mine 80% + 1 - 25 gold (random)
      • casino 50% (+ OR -) 40 - 50 gold (random)
